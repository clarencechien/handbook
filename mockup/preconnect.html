Ambient Journey

Kyoto Adventure

A detailed plan for our upcoming trip to the heart of Japan.

Welcome to your personalized travel handbook. This guide contains our full itinerary, including daily activities, important notes, and helpful links. Everything is available offline for your convenience.

Day 1: Arrival in Kyoto

  • Morning: Arrive at Kansai International Airport (KIX). Pick up Japan Rail Pass.
  • Afternoon: Take the Haruka Express to Kyoto Station. Check into our hotel near Gion.
  • Evening: Welcome dinner at a traditional Izakaya in Pontocho Alley.
Important Note: Keep your passport and JR Pass handy at all times.

Day 2: Temples and Traditions

  • Morning: Visit the iconic Fushimi Inari Shrine with its thousands of vermilion torii gates.
  • Afternoon: Explore the serene Arashiyama Bamboo Grove and the beautiful Tenryu-ji Temple.
  • Evening: Experience traditional Japanese performing arts at the Gion Corner Cultural Show.
